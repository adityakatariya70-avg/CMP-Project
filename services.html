Our Services

Explore the complaint services available for different public and community issues.

💧Water Supply

Report water leakage, pipeline damage, low water pressure, and interruptions in water supply.

⚡ Electricity Complaints

Report power outages, faulty meters, damaged electric poles, exposed wires, and other electricity-related issues.

🛣 Road & Infrastructure

Report damaged roads, potholes, broken streetlights, and public infrastructure issues.

🧹 Sanitation

Report garbage accumulation, drainage blockages, and sanitation-related concerns.

💡 Street Light

Report non-functional street lights, damaged poles, flickering lights, broken fixtures, and other street lighting issues affecting public safety.

🚌 Public Transport

Report transport delays, bus stop issues, traffic management problems, and other transport concerns.

🏛Government Office

Report issues related to government offices including delayed services, staff misconduct and public service grievances.

📋 Other Complaints

Submit complaints that do not fall under any predefined category for further review.
